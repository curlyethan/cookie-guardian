Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Overview

Cookie Guardian is a browser extension that automatically deletes cookies to protect your privacy. We are committed to transparency about how the extension works and what data it accesses.

Data Collection

Cookie Guardian does NOT collect any personal data.

What we DO NOT do:

  • We do NOT send any data to external servers
  • We do NOT track your browsing history
  • We do NOT collect analytics or telemetry
  • We do NOT require an account
  • We do NOT sell or share any data

Third-Party Services

If you choose to upgrade to Premium, payments are processed by ExtensionPay. We only receive confirmation that a purchase was completed - we do not see your payment card details.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage APIs. No data leaves your device except normal browser requests and payment verification (Premium only).

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on GitHub.

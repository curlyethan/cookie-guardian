Automatically delete cookies when you close browser tabs. Privacy without thinking about it.

Why Cookie Guardian?

Cookie AutoDelete was disabled by Chrome in December 2024, leaving 100,000+ users without an alternative. Cookie Guardian is built from the ground up for Manifest V3 - it won't get disabled.

Features

  • Auto-delete cookies when tab closes
  • Whitelist sites you trust (banks, email, etc.)
  • Works on ALL websites - no rules to maintain
  • Daily badge counter showing cookies deleted
  • Weekly summary notifications
  • Light/dark/system themes

Why Cookie Guardian?

Cookie AutoDelete was disabled by Chrome in December 2024, leaving 100,000+ users without a working alternative. Many "replacements" are buggy, abandoned, or don't actually delete cookies.

Cookie Guardian is built from the ground up for Manifest V3. It won't get disabled. It actually works. And it's actively maintained with regular updates.

We've fixed the bugs others haven't - cookies reliably delete when tabs close, statistics track correctly across browser restarts, and your whitelist is always respected.
